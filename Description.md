## Winter Enchantments
**Brings cold-weather content to Minecraft with enchantments focused on snow and frost mechanics. Discover new structures and trade with a winter-themed villager.**

---

**Enchantments**  
Can be obtained through trading, structure loot, and the enchanting table.

- **Frost Resistance** (chestplate) – Increases resistance to frost.  
  Level 1-3 | Incompatible with: Protection.

- **Frost Trap** (bow) – Temporarily spawns a powdered snow and ice trap upon hitting an enemy.  
  Level 1 | Incompatible with: Infinity and Mending.

- **Frostbite** (melee) – Applies a frost effect to enemies.  
  Level 1-3.

- **Frozen Stability** (leggings) – Prevents knockback while crouching.  
  Level 1.

- **Ice Claws** (boots) – Prevents slipping on ice.  
  Level 1.

- **Snow Cushion** (boots) – Reduces fall damage on snow.  
  Level 1-3 | Incompatible with: Snowdrift and Feather Falling.

- **Snow Hop** (boots) – Increases step height for players standing on snow.  
  Level 1-3.

- **Snow Plow** (shovel) – Moves snow from unwanted areas.  
  Level 1 | Incompatible with: Silk Touch and Fortune.

- **Snow Runner** (boots) – Increases movement speed while on snow.  
  Level 1-3 | Incompatible with: Soul Speed.

- **Snow Shoes** (boots) – Prevents sinking in powdered snow.  
  Level 1.

- **Snowdrift** (boots) – Creates temporary snow where the player walks.  
  Level 1-3.

**Structures**  
Adds homes for the Snow Scribes:

- **Snow Scribe House** – Snowy Plains and Snowy Taiga.
- **Snow Scribe Basement** – Snowy Plains.
- **Snow Scribe Iceberg** – Deep Ocean, Cold Ocean, and Deep Cold Ocean.

**Villager**  
Adds the Snow Scribe:

- **Workstation block:** Chiseled Bookshelf.
